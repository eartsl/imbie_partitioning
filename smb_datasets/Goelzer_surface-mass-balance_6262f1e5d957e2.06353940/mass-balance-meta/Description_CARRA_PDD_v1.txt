CARRA_PDD_1km

This submission makes use of a new reanalysis dataset for Greenland (CARRA, Schyberg et al., 2020). Since the reanalysis model does not produce its own surface mass balance, it has been augmented by a positive-degree-day (PDD) calculation (Janssens and Huybrechts, 2000; Gregory et al., 2006; Fürst et al., 2015) to estimate surface melt, retention and runoff. Because the reanalysis has a relatively high spatial resolution (2.5km x 2.5km) we force the PDD model directly with absolute 2m air temperature rather than with anomalies. We have tuned the parameter 'sigma' in the PDD model (Janssens and Huybrechts, 2000) to roughly match total Greenland surface mass balance to PROMICE reconstructions (Mankoff et al., 2021). The PDD model is run with annual time resolution on the 1km x 1km ISMIP6 grid.
